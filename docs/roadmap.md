# Gwen Beauty Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation (Stability & Speed)
**Focus:** app stability, performance, reliability.

- Architecture cleanup (folder structure, naming, state consistency)
- Global error handling (error/empty state consistency)
- Performance (lazy load, pagination, image caching, shimmer)
- Analytics basics (view product, add cart, checkout, purchase)
- Minimal unit tests (cart, wishlist, checkout logic)

**KPI:** crash-free > 99%, cold start < 2s, smooth scrolling.

---

## Phase 1 — Commerce Core (Must‑Have Conversion)
**Focus:** purchase flow reliability end‑to‑end.

- Cart flow stability (stock logic, price calculation, promo handling)
- Checkout + payment verification clarity
- Order success + order detail tracking
- Delivery ETA + fee transparency
- Refund/return info clarity

**KPI:** checkout drop rate down, payment success rate up.

---

## Phase 2 — Trust & Social Proof
**Focus:** build buyer confidence.

- Verified review badges
- Review filters (most helpful, verified)
- Rating breakdown + heatmap
- Brand/official seller badges
- Buyer protection copy

**KPI:** faster time to purchase, higher review engagement.

---

## Phase 3 — Discovery & Search Intelligence
**Focus:** faster product discovery.

- Search autosuggest + typo correction
- Smart filters (skin type, ingredients)
- “Best deal” sorting
- Personalized home (recently viewed, best match)
- Trending + best seller discovery

**KPI:** higher search → product click CTR.

---

## Phase 4 — Retention & Loyalty
**Focus:** bring users back.

- Wishlist price‑drop alerts
- Loyalty points / tier (starter)
- Daily check‑in / rewards
- Notification personalization

**KPI:** higher DAU/MAU, more wishlist conversion.

---

## Phase 5 — Content & Community
**Focus:** engagement & social reach.

- Shorts (UGC, creators)
- Editorial newsfeed
- Brand/creator profile
- Share + deep links

**KPI:** longer sessions, higher share rate.

---

## Phase 6 — Premium & Delight
**Focus:** luxury experience.

- Premium motion system (smooth transitions, hero parallax)
- Cinematic hero cards
- Consistent gold/teal accents
- Micro‑interactions (tap feedback, glow highlight)

**KPI:** higher UX NPS, positive sentiment.
